🍝 White Sauce Pasta Recipe

This White Sauce Pasta is a creamy and delicious Italian dish made with milk, butter, cheese, and herbs. It’s quick to make and perfect for lunch or dinner!

🧂 Ingredients

  • 1 cup pasta (penne or macaroni)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Mixed vegetables (like capsicum, corn, carrot)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until soft, then drain and keep aside.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and add flour. Stir well for a minute.
  3. Slowly pour in milk, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  4. Cook until the sauce thickens, then add cheese, salt, and pepper.
  5. Add boiled pasta and sautéed vegetables into the sauce.
  6. Mix well so the sauce coats the pasta evenly.
  7. Garnish with chili flakes and serve hot!

Tip: You can also add oregano or grated mozzarella for extra flavor.
