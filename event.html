🏮 Lantern Light Festival – China

Experience the magic of ancient China come alive under the night sky! The Lantern Light Festival is a beautiful celebration of history, art, and culture, filled with glowing lanterns, traditional performances, and delicious street food. Don’t miss this once-in-a-year night of lights and legends!

📅 Date & Time

Date: February 12, 2026
Time: 6:00 PM – 11:00 PM

📍 Location

Historic Xi’an City Wall, Shaanxi Province, China The festival will take place along the ancient city wall, surrounded by glowing lanterns and lively music.

🎆 Event Highlights

  • Traditional Chinese dance and music performances
  • Lantern-making workshops for families
  • Storytelling sessions about ancient Chinese myths
  • Food stalls serving authentic Chinese cuisine
  • Midnight fireworks over the city wall

🗓️ Agenda

  1. 6:00 PM – Opening Ceremony
  2. 7:00 PM – Cultural Performances
  3. 8:30 PM – Lantern Parade
  4. 10:00 PM – Fireworks Display
  5. 10:30 PM – Closing Speech
